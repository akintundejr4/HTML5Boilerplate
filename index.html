Orchestra of the Month

The New York Philharmonic Orchestra

  • Founded in 1842
  • Peforms in the storied Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts
  • Oldest Orchestra in the US
  • Conducted by Lorin Maazel
  • Routinely ranked in the top 5 worldwide.

Recognized Worlwide as one of the greatest symphony orchestra's of our time, the New York Philharmonic serves as the gold standard for quality in both sound and performance among classical musical followers around the world.

The New York Philharmonic plays a leading cultural role in New York City, the United States, and the world. Each season the Philharmonic connects with upt ot 50 million music lovers through live concerts in New York City and on worldwide tours.
