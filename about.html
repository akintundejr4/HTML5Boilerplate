About Orchestra Unlimited

To put it simply, Orchestra Unlimited is a collection of classical and contemporary music enthusiasts dedicated to the observation and characterization of what we believe to be the top five symphony orchestra's in the world.

Our members include but are not limited to several Music PhD holding professor's, leading industry composers, notable academia, contemporary classical composers and the board of trustees at the Julliard School of Music.

We are commited to the unbiased observation and ranking of the quality, frequency and integrity of the performances put forth by the symphony orchestra's on our list, as well as keeping tabs on potential additions to the list by maintaining observance of orchestra's around the world.
